We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted partners when necessary to provide our services — for example, payment processors or analytics tools — under strict confidentiality agreements.

You can access, update, or delete your data anytime by contacting our support team. You may also opt out of marketing emails by following the unsubscribe link in our messages.

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be announced on our website or via email.
